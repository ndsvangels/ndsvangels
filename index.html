Our Mission

The Notre Dame Silicon Valley Angels (NDSVA) is a collective of angels who are University of Notre Dame alumni, faculty, and friends based in the Bay Area and beyond. Our mission is to leverage our collective experience, network, and resources to support high-potential startups and foster innovation. We are passionate about helping great companies grow while strengthening the Notre Dame entrepreneurial ecosystem.

Our Approach

01

Identify

We look for opportunities with high potential and long-term growth, with a focus on Technology in the Bay Area, though we are open to opportunities globally.

02

Partner

We leverage prior investment experience, industry expertise, and functional expertise to partner with growing companies and entrepreneurs.

03

Invest

Members make individual investment decisions. We facilitate the process through due diligence, deal screening, and syndication opportunities. All investing members are accredited investors as defined in SEC Regulation D, Rule 501
