Join the Network

Become part of the Notre Dame angel investing & mentoring ecosystem.

NDSVA is a free, membership-based organization run entirely by volunteers with membership managed entirely via LinkedIn groups which we all use already. Simply request to join the Notre Dame Silicon Valley Angel Group on its LinkedIn page by clicking here. Once we have verified that you are a Notre Dame alumni, you will be admitted to the group. You can then see other members and build your entrepreneurial network.

We want to encourage the interaction of Notre Dame alumni dedicated to partnering with entrepreneurs who are changing the world in an open and collaborative way-- it's not easy to start a company and, as a group, we want to help. If you want to help mentor an entrepreneur, please first join the group, then fill out the mentor application below. If you're an entrepreneur and need help, just reach out via LinkedIn messaging to other members-- you will be amazed how helpful we all are as a community - GO IRISH!

Membership benefits include regular invites to our pitch events, the ability to apply to pitch yourself, and networking with fellow alumni. In order to make investments, you must be an accredited investor to join as an Angel Member.
