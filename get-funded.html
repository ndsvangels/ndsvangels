Get Funded

We are looking for the next generation of world-changing startups.

Application Process

We organize Pitch Nights 2 times per year (in-person in the Bay Area or virtual) but applications are reviewed on a rolling basis. Startups have 20 minutes for presentation and Q&A.

1

Apply Online

Complete our application form. It takes about 5-10 minutes. We want to know about your team, your product, and your traction. At least one of the founders must be a Notre Dame alum.

Apply Now
2

Screening

If your startup is a good fit, a member of our diligence team will reach out for a 30-minute screening call to learn more.

3

Pitch Night Selection

Selected companies are invited to pitch at our upcoming event. We'll work with you on scheduling and preparation.
